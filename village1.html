BARSU VILLAGE

Let us see know about Barsu village!!!

Barsu village is situated in Uttarkashi district amidst scenic surroundings. It is the starting point of the trek to Dyara Bugyal.Its a small and scenic hamlet situated on road side in Uttarkashi district known for the base camp of Dayara Bugyal.

Famous places at Barsu :

Dayara Bugyal Winter Trek -

Ideal trek for long weekends, Dayara Bugyal can be a perfect start for beginners. Dayara Bugyal trek goes through open land and dense forest at different places ending up at the vast expanse of lush green meadow. Most of the trekkers start their trek from Barsu village and they trek down via Raithal to see the scenic beauty of both the routes of Dayara Bugyal.


Gidara Bugyal - A Hidden Trek

Gidara Bugyal is situated around 20 kms beyond the famous Dayara Bugyal is yet to be explored. Not many have laid their eyes on this high altitude meadow, its time to set foot on it. While Dayara Bugyal makes an easy weekend trek, Gidara Bugyal is ideal for serious trekkers.

Famous local things at Barsu :

  • Nature made item or cosmetics
  • Chocolate factories
  • Sweet made of palm sugar

HARSIL VILLAGE

Let us see know about Harsil village!!!

Harsil is an unspoiled and hidden jewel of Uttarakhand state offering ample scope for people seeking peace and serenity in the lap of Himalayas.
